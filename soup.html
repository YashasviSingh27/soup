    Partner-distributors

    Our distributions are provided by more than 50 distributor partners located in all regions of Switzerland. You can thus order, without problem, your gases; they are delivered to you on the date of your choice.

    A wide range of gases from our distributor partners

    Our distributor partners have a wide range of gases. Industrial gases, refrigerants, propane, welding and cutting gases, Protadur® food gases and diving gases are available in almost all our depots.

    Specific needs or questions?

    Do you have specific needs, or do you want to order large quantities of (industrial) gas? Contact our logistics department. The specialists of Westfalen Gas Schweiz GmbH will be happy to advise you. We can adapt the stock of the deposit and deliveries to your needs. Westfalen Gas Schweiz GmbH therefore guarantees you a reliable gas supplier, near you.

     

    A depot near you

    Would you like to know the Westfalen Gas Schweiz GmbH distributor partner closest to you? Go to our Partner-Distributors search tab.
